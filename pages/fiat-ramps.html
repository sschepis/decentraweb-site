Fiat On/Off Ramps

Move between fiat currency and crypto via Circle API integration. Deposit USD to receive USDC, and withdraw USDC back to your bank account.

⚠
Fiat ramp operations require a completed KYC verification via SumSub or Persona and may be subject to jurisdictional restrictions.

Circle API Integration

Fiat on/off ramps are powered by the Circle API, providing USDC minting and redemption directly from bank accounts:

  • Bank Account Linking — Securely link US bank accounts via Plaid or wire transfer instructions. Bank account status and verification is tracked in the fiatStore
  • Fiat-to-Crypto (Deposit) — Initiate a deposit: select bank account, enter USD amount. Funds are withdrawn from the bank via ACH/wire, and USDC is minted to your wallet. Status tracking via TransferStatusCard
  • Crypto-to-Fiat (Withdraw) — Initiate a withdrawal: USDC is burned via Circle and USD is transferred to your linked bank account. Typically settles in 1-3 business days for ACH, same-day for wires

Transaction Lifecycle

  1. Initiate — User initiates deposit or withdrawal via the FiatRamps page
  2. Verify — KYC status and bank account are validated. For deposits, the platform verifies the bank account has sufficient funds
  3. Process — Circle processes the transfer. Status updates are pushed to the app in real-time
  4. Settle — On settlement, USDC is minted (deposit) or burned (withdrawal). A confirmation is shown with the transaction hash
