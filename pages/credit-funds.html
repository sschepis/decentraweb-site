Credit Scoring & Private Credit Funds

On-chain credit scoring with commitment privacy, plus private credit fund management for deposits, withdrawals, and deal tracking.

On-Chain Credit Scoring

The CreditScoringFacet (Diamond facet) enables trusted publishers to issue privacy-preserving credit scores on-chain using commitment hashing:

  • Commitment Privacy — Raw credit scores are never stored on-chain. Only a commitment hash Poseidon(score, address, salt) is published. The actual score can be selectively disclosed via ZK range proofs
  • Trusted Publishers — Only authorized publishers can issue credit scores. The setCreditPublisher() function controls the publisher registry
  • Credit Tiers — getCreditTier() resolves a commitment to a tier: Excellent (750+), Good (700-749), Fair (650-699), Poor (below 650)

Combined with ZK Attestations, users can prove "My credit tier is Excellent" without revealing their exact score or identity.

Credit Scoring Factors

Payment History

On-chain repayment track record from trade deals and credit fund instruments

Collateral Ratio

Active collateral positions across protocols, weighted by asset risk

Account Age

Identity and wallet age on Optimism, factoring in activity consistency

KYC Status

Verified KYC claims from trusted issuers boost creditworthiness

Private Credit Funds

A dedicated application for managing private credit investments, built on the Diamond proxy with 3 dedicated facets:

FacetPurpose
PrivateCreditFundManagementFacetFund creation, capital calls, NAV reporting, investor whitelisting
PrivateCreditFundOperationsFacetDeposits, withdrawals, interest distribution, fee collection
PrivateCreditFundDistributionFacetRevenue waterfall for fund distributions, carried interest calculation

Fund Management Features

  • Fund Creation — Create credit funds with configurable parameters: target raise, minimum investment, lockup period, management fee, carried interest
  • Investor Dashboard — View active funds, deposited capital, accrued interest, and pending distributions
  • Deal Tracking — Fund managers publish deals, track repayment schedules, and report performance. Deals integrate with the Trade Finance system
  • Interest Distribution — Automated interest distribution to investors based on pro-rata share and waterfall configuration

Collateral Token Integration

Credit funds can use RestrictedCollateralTokens issued via the CollateralTokenFactoryFacet. These are compliance-gated ERC-20 tokens that restrict transfers to verified identities, ensuring regulatory compliance for private fund interests.

Key Components

CreditScoringFacet

Diamond facet: publish score commitments, resolve tiers, manage publishers

creditScoringService

TypeScript service: score resolution, tier calculation, publisher verification

privateCreditFundService

Fund CRUD, deposit/withdrawal flows, deal tracking, distributions

CreditFundsApp

Pre-built UI application: fund listing, investor dashboard, deposit/withdrawal forms
