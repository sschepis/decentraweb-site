DAO Governance

Full governance framework with proposals, voting strategies, delegation, and treasury management.

Overview

Every domain in DecentraClick can enable DAO governance, turning the organization into a decentralized autonomous organization with on-chain proposals, voting, and treasury execution.

Proposal Types

TypeDescriptionExample
GeneralNon-binding governance proposals"Should we partner with Project X?"
TreasuryProposals to spend from the treasury"Allocate 1000 USDC for marketing"
MembershipAdd or remove members"Add alice.eth as a contributor"
ParameterChange governance parameters"Reduce quorum from 51% to 40%"
UpgradeSmart contract upgrades"Add the MarketplaceFacet to our Diamond"

Voting Strategies

  • Token-Weighted — Voting power proportional to token holdings
  • NFT-Gated — One vote per qualifying NFT held
  • One-Person-One-Vote — Equal voting power for all members
  • Quadratic — Square root of token holdings for more equitable representation

Governance Lifecycle

  1. Proposal Creation — A member creates a proposal with title, description, and execution plan
  2. Discussion Period — Members discuss the proposal before voting opens
  3. Voting Period — Members cast their votes within the configured time window
  4. Quorum Check — Verify minimum participation threshold is met
  5. Execution — If passed, the proposal is executed (treasury transfers, parameter changes, etc.)

Delegation

Members can delegate their voting power to other members. Delegation is revocable at any time and does not transfer tokens.

Configuration

Quorum

Minimum percentage of voting power that must participate (default: 51%)

Voting Period

Duration that voting is open (configurable days)

Proposal Threshold

Minimum tokens/reputation to create proposals

Execution Delay

Timelock between vote passing and execution
