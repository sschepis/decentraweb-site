Interoperability Bridges

Cross-protocol bridges for ENS, Lens, Ceramic, XMTP, and The Graph integration.

Overview

DecentraClick connects with the broader Web3 ecosystem through interoperability bridges. These bridges sync data, resolve identities, and extend platform functionality across multiple protocols.

Supported Bridges

ProtocolBridge TypeCapabilities
ENSIdentity & NamingResolve ENS names to .dnet domains, sync ENS records
Lens ProtocolSocial GraphImport Lens profiles, sync followers and publications
Ceramic/ComposeDBData ModelsSync data models between Ceramic and DDB
XMTPMessagingBridge messages between XMTP and the platform email service
The GraphIndexingAuto-generate subgraphs from deployed contracts

Cross-Chain Bridging

The Bridge Service enables asset transfers between supported chains:

  • Optimism ↔ Ethereum — Native L1/L2 bridging
  • Multi-chain Assets — Transfer tokens across supported networks
  • State Sync — Synchronize contract state across chains
