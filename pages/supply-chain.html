Supply Chain

End-to-end supply chain tracking with on-chain provenance, milestones, and trade finance integration.

Overview

The Supply Chain service enables transparent tracking of goods from origin to destination. Events are recorded on-chain for immutable provenance, with integration into the trade finance and escrow modules.

Features

  • Shipment Tracking — Track goods through waypoints from origin to destination
  • Milestone Events — Record key events (pickup, customs, delivery) with timestamps
  • On-chain Provenance — Immutable audit trail via SupplyChainRegistry
  • Trade Deal Integration — Link supply chain events to trade finance deals
  • Document Attachment — Attach shipping documents, invoices, and certificates
  • Temperature/Condition Monitoring — Track environmental conditions during transit
