Compliance & KYC

Compliance screening, sanctions checking, KYC marketplace, and regulatory reporting.

Overview

The Compliance service provides regulatory compliance tools including sanctions screening, KYC verification, and audit trails. It integrates with the identity and attestation systems for a comprehensive compliance framework.

Features

  • Sanctions Screening — Check addresses and entities against sanctions lists
  • KYC Marketplace — Multiple KYC providers compete for verification services
  • Compliance Registry — On-chain compliance status tracking
  • Attestation Integration — KYC results become verifiable attestations
  • Credit Scoring — Automated credit assessment and underwriting
  • Audit Trail — Complete record of all compliance checks and decisions
  • Watch List Monitoring — Ongoing monitoring of addresses against updated lists

KYC Flow

  1. Request — Organization or user initiates KYC verification
  2. Provider Selection — Choose from marketplace providers based on price, speed, jurisdiction
  3. Verification — SumSub or other provider performs identity verification
  4. Attestation — Successful verification creates an on-chain attestation
  5. Access Grant — Compliance status unlocks restricted features
