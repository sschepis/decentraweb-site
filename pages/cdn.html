CDN Service

Content delivery network with edge caching, IPFS integration, and domain-scoped content management.

Overview

The CDN Service provides content delivery and caching for DecentraClick applications. It manages content distribution across IPFS and edge nodes with configurable caching strategies.

Features

  • Edge Caching — Cache content at edge nodes for fast delivery
  • IPFS Integration — Pin and serve content via IPFS
  • Cache Strategies — Network-first, cache-first, and stale-while-revalidate
  • Domain Scoping — Content scoped to specific domain organizations
  • Purge Controls — Manual and automatic cache invalidation
  • Metrics — Hit rates, bandwidth, and latency monitoring
