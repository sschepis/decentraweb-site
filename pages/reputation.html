Reputation System

On-chain reputation scoring with multi-dimensional metrics, history tracking, and trust graphs.

Overview

The Reputation System provides a multi-dimensional scoring framework for users and organizations. Scores are computed from on-chain activity, peer endorsements, and verified credentials.

Score Dimensions

  • Transaction History — Score based on successful on-chain transactions
  • Peer Endorsements — Ratings from other platform participants
  • Governance Participation — Voting frequency and proposal quality
  • Compliance Score — KYC completion and compliance status
  • Service Reliability — Uptime and performance for service providers
  • Escrow Completion — Successful escrow deal completion rate

Use Cases

  • Governance Weight — Reputation can influence voting power
  • Access Control — Minimum reputation for certain features
  • Trust Scoring — Counterparty risk assessment in trade finance
  • Marketplace Rankings — Seller/buyer reputation for marketplace transactions
