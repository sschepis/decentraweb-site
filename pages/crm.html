CRM

Customer relationship management with domain-scoped contacts, interaction tracking, and pipeline management.

Overview

The CRM service provides contact and relationship management for organizations. Contacts, interactions, and pipelines are stored in the domain namespace with P2P synchronization.

Features

  • Contact Management — Store and manage contacts with rich profiles
  • Interaction Tracking — Log meetings, calls, emails, and notes
  • Pipeline Management — Track deals and opportunities through stages
  • Domain Scoped — CRM data isolated to each organization
  • Wallet Integration — Link contacts to blockchain addresses
  • On-chain Registry — Anchor key contact data on-chain via CRMRegistry
