Token Launchpad

Launch and distribute tokens with configurable sale types, vesting schedules, and airdrop campaigns. Built on the Diamond proxy with the MultiSale and VariablePrice facets.

Launch Types

Sale TypeDiamond FacetDescription
Fixed PriceMultiSaleFacetTokens sold at a fixed price. Configurable start/end time, caps per wallet and total
Variable PriceVariablePriceFacetDynamic pricing via bonding curves or configurable price schedules
Multi-Token SaleMultiSaleFacetSale of multiple token types (ERC-721/ERC-1155) in a single campaign

Features

  • Vesting Schedules — Configure cliff, linear, and stepped vesting for team, advisor, and investor allocations. Vesting is enforced on-chain via the Diamond
  • Airdrop Campaigns — Batch airdrop tokens to lists of addresses. Supports Merkle tree airdrops for gas-efficient distribution to large recipient sets
  • KYC-Gated Launches — Require KYC verification before participation. Integrates with the platform's identity and compliance system
  • Fee Configuration — Primary sale fees, secondary trade royalties, and creator earnings are managed through the FeeManagerFacet
  • Launch Registry — Each launch is registered on-chain via the LaunchpadRegistry contract with metadata, status, and participation records

Variable Pricing Models

The VariablePriceFacet supports multiple pricing strategies:

Linear Bonding Curve

Price increases linearly with tokens sold: P = base + slope * supply

Exponential Curve

Price grows exponentially: P = base * e^(k * supply)

Time-Based Dutch Auction

Price decreases from ceiling to floor over a configurable period

Custom Schedule

Arbitrary price points at configurable supply or time milestones

Token Presets

Launch any of the 10 Diamond token presets: Open NFT (ERC-721A), Permissioned NFT, Open Multi-Token (ERC-1155A), Fungible Token (ERC-20), NFT Marketplace, Nestable NFT (ERC-6059), Trade Finance Diamond, Gemforce NFT, Custom Diamond, or Stablecoin ARE.
