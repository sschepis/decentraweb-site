Trade Finance

Trade deal lifecycle management with approval queues, token issuance, and supply chain integration.

Overview

The Trade Finance module enables organizations to manage trade deals from inception through settlement. It includes approval workflows, token issuance for trade receivables, and integration with the supply chain module.

Features

  • Deal Lifecycle — Create, negotiate, approve, execute, and settle trade deals
  • Approval Queues — Multi-stage approval workflows with role-based authorization
  • Token Issuance — Issue tradeable tokens representing trade receivables (TSC model)
  • Private Credit — Create and manage private credit funds backed by trade receivables
  • Credit Scoring — Automated credit scoring and underwriting for counterparties
  • Supply Chain Link — Connect trade deals to supply chain tracking events

Diamond Facets

Trade finance is implemented through four specialized Diamond facets:

TradeDealAdminFacet

Deal creation and administrative operations

TradeDealManagementFacet

Deal negotiation and lifecycle management

TradeDealOperationsFacet

Deal execution and settlement

TradeDealApprovalQueueFacet

Multi-stage approval workflows

tradeFinanceService

Trade deal CRUD and lifecycle operations (14KB)

privateCreditFundService

Private credit fund management (11KB)

creditScoringService

Credit scoring and underwriting (17KB)
