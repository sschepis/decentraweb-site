Identity & Authentication

Decentralized identity management with ERC-1056, verifiable credentials, KYC integration, and multi-wallet support.

Identity System

DecentraClick implements a comprehensive decentralized identity system based on the ERC-1056 standard. Every user has a self-sovereign identity that they control through their wallet.

Core Features

  • ERC-1056 Identity Registry — On-chain identity anchoring with delegate and attribute management
  • Verifiable Credentials — Issue and verify claims about identity attributes
  • Multi-Wallet Linking — Link multiple wallet addresses to a single identity
  • Identity Claims — Structured claims with claim topics and trusted issuer registries
  • Guardian Recovery — Designate guardians who can help recover access to your identity
  • Profile Management — Rich user profiles stored on Gun.js with P2P replication

KYC/AML Integration

DecentraClick integrates with SumSub for Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) verification. The KYC Marketplace allows multiple providers to compete for verification services.

  • KYC Marketplace — Multiple KYC providers can offer verification services
  • Provider Dashboard — KYC providers manage their verification workflows
  • Bid System — Organizations can request KYC bids from multiple providers
  • Attestation Integration — KYC results become on-chain attestations

Attestation System

The attestation service allows trusted issuers to make verifiable claims about identities. Claims are organized by topic and can be verified on-chain.

  • Claim Topics Registry — Define categories of claims (e.g., age verification, accreditation)
  • Trusted Issuers Registry — Whitelist entities authorized to issue specific claim types
  • On-chain Verification — Claims can be verified directly via smart contracts
  • Claim Badges — Visual representation of verified claims on user profiles
