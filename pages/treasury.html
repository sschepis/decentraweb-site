Treasury Management

Multi-sig organization treasury with spending proposals, budgets, and approval workflows.

Overview

Every domain organization has a built-in treasury for managing shared funds. The treasury integrates with DAO governance for spending approval and provides transparent financial management.

Features

  • Multi-token Support — Hold ETH, ERC-20 tokens, and NFTs in the treasury
  • Spending Proposals — All withdrawals require governance approval via treasury proposals
  • Budget Allocation — Allocate budgets to departments or projects via subdomains
  • Transaction History — Complete audit trail of all treasury transactions
  • Balance Tracking — Real-time balance tracking across all held assets

Spending Workflow

  1. Create Treasury Proposal — Specify recipient, amount, token, and justification
  2. DAO Vote — Members vote on the spending proposal
  3. Execution — If approved, funds are transferred to the recipient
  4. Audit Trail — Transaction recorded on-chain with proposal reference
