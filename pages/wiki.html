Wiki

Collaborative knowledge base with versioning, spaces, and P2P synchronization.

Overview

The Wiki service provides collaborative documentation and knowledge management for organizations. Pages are stored on Gun.js with P2P replication, version history, and domain-scoped access control.

Features

  • Wiki Spaces — Organize pages into spaces by topic or team
  • Rich Text Editing — Markdown-based content with rich formatting
  • Version History — Track all changes with diff comparison
  • P2P Sync — Pages sync across peers via Gun.js
  • Access Control — Per-page and per-space RBAC permissions
  • Search — Full-text search across all wiki content
  • On-chain Anchoring — Anchor important pages on-chain via WikiRegistry
